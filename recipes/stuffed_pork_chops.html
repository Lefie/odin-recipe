Stuffed Pork Chop

Description

When I want comfort food, I make these deconstructed-style stuffed pork chops.
My husband loves the stuffing so much I usually double the recipe for the bread crumb
mixture and leave everything else the same.

The prep time is 20 mins and the cook time is 50 mins. The serving is
for 4 people

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Pork Chops
  • Bread cubes
  • Butter
  • Broth
  • Broth
  • Vegetables
  • Seasoning
  • Soup
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook the pork chops until brown on both sides.
  2. Make the stuffing, then spoon it in heaping mounds onto the pork chops.
  3. Combine the soup and water, then pour the mixture over the pork chops.
  4. Cover with foil and bake for half an hour, then uncover and continue baking.
